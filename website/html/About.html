Letflix is a well-known cinema company, founded in October 1977 by Joan Phoenix in Indonesia.
Letflix has opened many branches of international cinema in the world. By prioritizing high-quality
dramas and advances in digital technology, Letflix has experienced very rapid growth because it is liked
by many customers. Letflix decided to open a digital streaming service in 2011. In 2012, Letflix received
the award as the best digital drama streaming service company and streaming service with the most subscribers in the world.
